Make

Record, upload, cut, crop, trim, make GIFs, and more. Not sure where to start? Creating content is easier than ever with our AI script generator and AI-powered editing. And you can always elevate your video by adding interactive, clickable elements.

Edit

You can always trim, crop, and resize your content too. Make your videos shoppable and clickable by adding interactive elements like polls and CTAs. Keep your content organized in a beautiful video library. And, thanks to auto-transcription, search by caption anytime. Simplify the review process with time-coded notes, frame by frame. Collaborate with specific folks, put your video behind a password, or hide it from everyone but you.
